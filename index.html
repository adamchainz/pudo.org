Software projects

Current

  • 2013 twindle, client toolkit for the Twitter streaming API, storing and analysing statuses.
  • 2012-2013 dataset, easy-to-use Python library for data processing using relational databases.
  • 2011-2013 OffenesParlament, legislative activity tracking system for both chambers of the German parliament. Features plenary speeches, legislative drafts and personal profiles.
  • 2012-2013 nomenklatura, web-based data cleaning tool to help match aliases of an entity in source data and to map them to its correct form.
  • 2013 databin, a pastebin site for tabular data, enables easy sharing of small data snippets.
  • 2011-2013 ReGENESIS, data extraction from the repository of the German statistical office, data cube reconstruction.

Previous

  • 2010-2013 OpenSpending. Data warehousing system to support the analysis and visualization of budgetary and spending data from across the globe. Set up budget sites for Cameroon, Slovakia, Nepal, Bosnia and Minas Gerais (Brasil). Contributed to Where Does My Money Go? and the data.gov.uk spend browser.
  • 2011-2013 messytables, extracting structured data from badly-formatted tabular data.
  • 2011-2013 LobbyFacts API, an API to provide clean and current data on lobby activity in the European Union, cooperation with Corporate Europe, LobbyControl and Friends of the Earth Europe.
  • 2011 WebStore, RESTful, SQL-based data store and API generator service.
  • 2011 YourTopia, contributor, voting on a custom index for human development criteria. Third price in the World Bank's Apps for Development competition.
  • 2011 FlockScrape, distributed scraping of large web sites, e.g. the German companies register.
  • 2011 DataHub, light-weight, experimental, activity-centric clone of CKAN.
  • 2011 OffenerHaushalt, visualization of scraped German budget data.
  • 2010-2011 CKAN, worked on customizations for PublicData.eu, IATI Registry, Helsinki Regional Infoshare, the Data Hub and various community instances, including OffeneDaten.de in Germany.
  • 2009-2011 Adhocracy, collaborative policy drafting platform for distributed groups. Deployments for the Commission Internet and Digital Society of the German Parliament, the City of Munich and various other organisations.

Training and materials

  • 2012-2013 World Bank Institute Data Bootcamps, series of multi-day data literacy workshops for journalists, activists and coders in developing nations. Data trainer in Nairobi, Kenya, Grahamstown, South Africa, Chisinau, Moldova and Kathmandu, Nepal.
  • 2011-2013 School of Data Journalism, series of training events with the OKFN and European Journalism Centre. Contributed to workshops in Warsaw, Utrecht, Perugia, Vienna and Konjic (BiH).
  • 2012 Spending Data Handbook, book sprint to create guidance for civil society organisations in their use and advocacy of government financial data.
  • 2011-2012 Data Journalism Handbook, contributed chapters on data acquistion and produced a web version of the book.
  • 2011-2012 Open Data FAQ, frequently asked questions on the progress of open data initiatives in Germany and the relevant political and technical background.
  • 2010-2011 DataPatterns, a collection of design patterns for data processing. Deprecated in favor of the School of Data Handbook.
